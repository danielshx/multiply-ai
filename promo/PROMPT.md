## 🎬 Master prompt (copy everything below this line)

Cinematic 30-second tech-product promo. Denis Villeneuve meets Apple keynote. Arri Alexa 65, anamorphic 2.39:1, 24fps, shallow depth of field, crushed blacks, volumetric violet light, drifting dust motes.

**0–3s:** macro of a lone white particle in pitch-black void, pulsing then blooming into a violet corona. Anamorphic flare. Sub-bass hum.

**3–7s:** slow dolly-in on an empty executive desk at 2am — ringing desk phone, cold coffee, laptop looping "Follow up?". Teal-orange grade. The phone glows violet from within.

**7–10s:** violet floods the room. Time stops, one dust mote hovers in a god-ray. Ultra-thin white text lands: "Until now."

**10–14s:** void-black cut. "multiply" ignites letter-by-letter in bold white, each glyph shedding a violet shockwave ring. Subtitle "THE SWARM OUTREACH ENGINE" in wide-spaced mono. Faint scanlines.

**14–18s:** pull back to a living neural diagram — pulsing core orbited by 7 satellite orbs labeled Prospector · Researcher · Qualifier · Negotiator · Closer · Signal Hunter · Personaliser. Violet-to-cyan glow. Camera arcs around it.

**18–21s:** god's-eye descent into a dark control room, 25 holographic terminals in a 5×5 grid, each showing a live audio waveform and a mode badge flipping COLD → WARM → HOT → BOOKED.

**21–25s:** the grid dissolves into particles and rushes outward, revealing a slowly rotating dark Earth in space, Europe facing camera, clouds lit from the side. From a single violet node in Munich, 25 luminous signal arcs launch simultaneously — thin violet beams curving across the globe to 25 dots scattered across DACH, Italy, France, UK. 5 of the dots ignite gold and pulse (live connects). Tiny waveforms ride the arcs. Stripe-style atmosphere, volumetric glow on the earth's limb. Cinematic orbit camera.

**25–27s:** camera pulls through one of the gold pulses into a galaxy of 94 glowing nodes linked by 157 luminous edges, self-organizing. Brain firing.

**27–29s:** four massive kinetic numbers — **25 · 5 · 1 · 12** — DIALS · CONNECTS · MEETINGS · LEARNINGS. Each lands with a violet flash + sub-boom.

**29–30s:** final card — "multiply" in bright white on black, URL in violet mono below. Cut.

**Palette:** #000 void · #7c5cff hero violet · #5ce6d4 cool · #ffb347 warm · #ff5c7a hot · gold #ffd27a for live signals · pure white.

**Sound:** Hans Zimmer *Dune* × Trent Reznor *Social Network* × M83 *Outro*. 72 BPM minor → major at 10s. Sub hum, title boom, 25 L-R-panned UI chirps, whoosh as arcs launch, 5 gold impact-blips when calls land, data-stutter into graph, SMS-blip per KPI, final boom with reverb tail.

**Mood:** Blade Runner 2049 cool. Fortune 500 AI launch by an arthouse filmmaker.

## Tool-specific tips

- **Sora / Veo 3:** paste the full master prompt — both handle length + audio cues.

- **Pika:** keep under 80 words per clip. Append `cinematic, 24fps, anamorphic, shallow dof, crushed blacks` every time.
- **Midjourney V6 + video:** use the palette + mood paragraph as style reference for keyframes.
